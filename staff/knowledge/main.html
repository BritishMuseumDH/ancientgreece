The Knowledge and Learning chapter introduces some of the great thinkers and artists of ancient Greece.

The home page describes some of the achievements of the ancient Greeks and points to the impact these ancient thinkers have had on our modern day world.


Plato himself explains the allegory of the 'Myth of the Cave' in the Story section. Difficult concepts are made accessible with the use of animation.


The Explore presents information about some of the great thinkers and artists of ancient Greece. Material is organized by individual or by specialist field including art, literature, philosophy or politics.

The 'Plague of Athens' challenge asks children to match people's beliefs about disease to the suggested cure for the plague. Based on actual records made by the historian Thucydides, children interact with contemporary reactions to the plague which hit Athens between 430 and 426 BC.
