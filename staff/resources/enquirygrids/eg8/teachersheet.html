Enquiry Grid 8

Trading goods

Chapter/s and section/s used:
Geography challenge & Athens explore

Individual project:
Give each child a copy of the attached worksheet. Ask them to look through the Geography challenge and 'Piraeus' section of the Athens Explore. They need to find out if the good that is being traded is a local product or has to be exported. Ask them to fill in the worksheet.

After they have completed the task you could discuss how methods of transport could make the object more expensive.
