Pythagoras
c.570 - 480 BC

Pythagoras made very important discoveries in maths and music. He gave his name to a formula that is used to calculate the length of sides of triangles. He also used maths and numbers to explain musical notes.

He set up a religious society in southern Italy. He taught his followers to lead good and pure lives. He believed this would help them to live forever.
