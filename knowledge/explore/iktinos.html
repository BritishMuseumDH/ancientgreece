Iktinos
5th century BC

Iktinos was one of the architects of the Parthenon in Athens. He also worked on the sanctuary of Demeter at Eleusis.
This marble block comes from the Temple of Apollo Epikourios at Bassae, designed by Iktinos. The frieze shows Herakles and the Greeks fighting the Amazons.
