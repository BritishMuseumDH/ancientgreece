assest editing
______________


small images of objects
------------------------

1. open the dynamicLinkLibrary.fla
2. import the image into the library - file/import to library
3. go into the relevant movie clip for the imported image.- double click movieclip
4. drag image from library into the movie clip.
5. press ctrl & k to reveal the 'align panel'
6. make sure to stage: is clicked
7. select image & align to bottom edge & horizontal centre
8. click scene to return to the main timeline
