The Sanctuaries of Pandion and Zeus Polieus

Date: earlier structures possibly remodelled in the 440s BC
Architect: Unknown

 

Some of the ruins on the Acropolis give us a clear picture of how a particular building would have looked, whilst others leave many questions unanswered.

These two sanctuaries have been identified through archaeological work, and only foundations survive today.
