How do we know?

Herodotus' 'Histories' were written as a record of the victory of freedom-loving Greeks over the Persians. Herodotus was Greek himself and wrote a pro-Greek interpretation of the Persian Wars. An account of the wars written by a Persian might have provided us with a different picture of these events. We should keep this in mind when using Herodotus as a source on the Persian Wars.
