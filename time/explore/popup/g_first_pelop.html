460 - 446 BC; First Peloponnesian War

Hostilities flared between Sparta and Athens for many decades, notably between 460 and 446 BC, and later, between 431 and 404 BC.

At the end of the first Peloponnesian War, Sparta and Athens agreed a truce called the Thirty Years' Treaty, but hostilities broke out again 433 BC when Athens violated the terms.
