Pheidias active

Pheidias was considered to be one of the greatest Greek sculptors. He was famous for his gold and ivory statue of Athena Promachos on the Athenian Akropolis and the huge cult statue of Zeus at Olympia.

He was a friend of Perikles and played an important role in the rebuilding of Athens during the 440s and 430s. Pheidias died around 432 BC.
