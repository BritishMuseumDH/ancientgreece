Zeno founds Stoicism

Stoicism is a special type of philosophy. It gets its name from the Stoa Poikile, an open hall with columns in the Athenian Agora. It was in this Stoa that Zeno taught his followers. Zeno and the Stoics believed that only the very wise could be said to be truly good. They also believed that people should avoid harmful emotions like fear or envy.
