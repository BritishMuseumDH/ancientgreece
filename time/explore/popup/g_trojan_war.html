Traditional date of the Trojan War

The Trojan War was a legendary battle between a number of Greek kings and the city of Troy. The Greeks besieged Troy for 10 years. The war was traditionally said to have started when the Trojan prince Paris abducted Helen, the wife of King Menelaus of Sparta. Part of the war is described in Homer’s Iliad.

The Greek hero, Achilles, fights the Amazon queen who is helping the Trojans.
