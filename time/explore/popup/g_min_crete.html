Minoan civilization in Crete between 3200 BC and 1200 BC

The Minoan civilization emerged in Crete around 3200 BC, and flourished especially from around 2000 BC onwards. The Minoans built a number of grand palaces on Crete from which they governed the island. These palaces were often richly decorated with frescoes. Objects excavated from Minoan towns show the skill of their artists and craftsmen. The civilization grew rich through trade with Greece, Egypt and the Levant.
