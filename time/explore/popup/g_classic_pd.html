Classical period

During the Classical period artists began to portray humans and animals in a more natural way. This was a golden age for Greek arts and learning. Many famous playwrights, philosophers and sculptors lived at this time.

Democracy was developed in Athens and a number of other Greek cities adopted this type of government.

At the end of the Classical period the Macedonian Kingdom conquered and united the Greek cities and the Macedonian King Alexander the Great created an empire that stretched from Greece to Egypt and India.
