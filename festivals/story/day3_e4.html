Day 3: The banquet



A cup showing a banquet scene

Once more the evening is a time for celebrating the victories of the day. The meat from the morning's sacrifice is divided between athletes, special guests and spectators for a huge banquet. The ambassadors who had paraded around the Altis in the morning now make use of the gold and silver vessels for the banquet, and so display the wealth of their city.
